# Business Context: GreenWave Analytics Ltd.

## Company Profile

**Company Name:** GreenWave Analytics Ltd.

**Location:** Dublin, Ireland (EU-based)

**Industry:** Software as a Service (SaaS)

**Business Model:** B2B SaaS - Data Analytics Dashboard Platform

**Target Customers:** Small to Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) across multiple sectors

## Core Business Operations

### What We Do
GreenWave Analytics provides cloud-hosted data analytics dashboards that enable SMEs to:
- Collect and visualize business data
- Generate actionable insights from their operations
- Make data-driven business decisions
- Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) in real-time

### Technology Infrastructure
- **Cloud Platform:** AWS (Amazon Web Services)
- **Application Architecture:** Web-based SaaS application
- **Internal Systems:** Employee laptops running corporate IT environment
- **Data Storage:** Customer data hosted in AWS S3 and RDS databases

## Data Handling & Privacy

### Types of Data Processed
1. **Customer Personal Data**
   - User account information (names, emails, phone numbers)
   - User roles and permissions
   - Subject to GDPR regulations

2. **Customer Business Data**
   - Business metrics, sales data, operational KPIs
   - Client-specific analytics and reports
   - Confidential business information

3. **Employee Data**
   - Internal staff information
   - Access credentials and system logs

### Data Sensitivity Classification
- **High Sensitivity:** Customer personal data, financial information
- **Medium Sensitivity:** Business usage data, internal communications
- **Low Sensitivity:** Public-facing documentation, general marketing materials

## Regulatory & Compliance Obligations

### GDPR Compliance
- Must ensure lawful basis for data processing
- Required to implement data protection by design
- Must facilitate data subject rights (access, deletion, portability)
- Mandatory breach notification within 72 hours
- Data Processing Agreements (DPA) with all customers

### ISO 27001 Alignment
- Implementing ISO 27001:2013/2022 aligned security controls
- Information Security Management System (ISMS) in place
- Regular security assessments and audits
- Risk management framework aligned with ISO standards

## Business Priorities & Risk Priorities

### Priority 1: Data Confidentiality
**Why:** Customer data is our most valuable asset. Any unauthorized access could result in:
- Loss of customer trust
- Regulatory fines under GDPR
- Reputational damage
- Loss of business

### Priority 2: Service Availability
**Why:** Our business model depends on 24/7 platform availability:
- Downtime directly impacts customer operations
- SLA commitments (e.g., 99.9% uptime) are contractual obligations
- Service disruptions lead to revenue loss and customer churn

### Priority 3: Data Integrity
**Why:** Analytics accuracy is critical to customer decision-making:
- Data corruption could lead to incorrect business decisions
- Impacts trust in our platform
- Could result in customer liability claims

### Priority 4: Customer Trust & Reputation
**Why:** Security posture directly influences market competitiveness:
- Customers require proof of security certifications
- Security incidents damage brand reputation
- Required for enterprise customer contracts
