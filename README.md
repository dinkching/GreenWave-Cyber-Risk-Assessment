## Overview
This project simulates a real-world cybersecurity risk assessment conducted for an EU-based SaaS organisation operating under GDPR obligations.

The objective was to identify critical assets, analyse threats, assess cybersecurity risks, and recommend appropriate security controls aligned with ISO/IEC 27001 and NIST principles.

## Scope
- Cloud-hosted SaaS application
- Customer personal and business data
- Internal employee systems
- Third-party cloud services

## Methodology
- Asset Identification & Classification
- Threat Modelling using STRIDE
- Risk Assessment based on Likelihood & Impact
- Risk Prioritisation using a Risk Matrix
- Control Mapping to ISO/IEC 27001 Annex A

## Key Outcomes
- Identified high-risk threats impacting data confidentiality and service availability
- Developed a structured risk register with mitigation strategies
- Mapped security risks to internationally recognised security controls
- Produced executive-level reporting for non-technical stakeholders

## Deliverables
- Asset Register and classification
- STRIDE-based threat model and data flow diagram
- Quantified cybersecurity risk register and risk heat map
- ISO/IEC 27001 control mapping with mitigation prioritisation
- Executive-level cybersecurity risk summary
