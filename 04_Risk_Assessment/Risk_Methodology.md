# Risk Assessment Methodology

This document describes the methodology used for conducting the cyber risk assessment.

## Assessment Approach

### 1. Risk Identification
- Systematic review of organizational assets
- Threat analysis using STRIDE methodology
- Vulnerability assessment of systems

### 2. Risk Analysis
- Likelihood evaluation (High, Medium, Low)
- Impact assessment (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
- Risk scoring calculation

### 3. Risk Evaluation
- Risk prioritization matrix
- Threshold-based acceptance decisions
- Stakeholder consultation
