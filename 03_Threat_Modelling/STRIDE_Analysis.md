# STRIDE Analysis

Systematic threat modeling analysis using STRIDE methodology (Spoofing, Tampering, Repudiation, Information Disclosure, Denial of Service, Elevation of Privilege).

## Threats Identified

- Spoofing: User identity spoofing attacks
- Tampering: Data integrity threats
- Repudiation: Denial of actions
- Information Disclosure: Unauthorized data access
- Denial of Service: Service disruption
- Elevation of Privilege: Unauthorized access escalation
